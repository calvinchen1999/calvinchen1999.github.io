About Us

DeCign is a web system that designed to discover designers’ ideas, mainly using images and shorter articles to share creativity with every designers. The site was founded by Calvin Chen in March of 2018, with the goal to “Share the Designs”. After putting into use, DeCign is keeping its purpose to construct a “World-Wide Design Ecosphere”, providing designers a platform to share, learn, show and communicate.

DeCign is a leading online platform to showcase & discover creative work, to remove the barriers between talent & opportunity. The creative world updates their work in one place to broadcast it widely and efficiently. Companies explore the work and access talent on a global scale.

We are a bootstrapped and profitable company helping the world’s design talent share their creations and get hired. DeCign has become a go-to resource for discovering and connecting with designers and creative talent around the globe. We also help some of the world’s best design-forward companies including Apple, Airbnb, IDEO, Facebook, Google, Dropbox, Slack, Shopify, Lyft get exposure for their design teams and to help them hire expert creatives.

We appreciate every user who is interested in and loves DeCign. We wish everyone who joins us can enjoy the delights of design and share.
